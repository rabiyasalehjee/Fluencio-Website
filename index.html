About Fluencio

We aim to help people overcome the fear of public speaking and stage fright. Being able to communicate and interact with people is an important social skill yet a lot of people are sfacing trouble in public speaking. Fluencio - an android based mobile application is now at your service to communicate with confidence and improve your public speaking skill.
Our vision regarding Fluencio is ambitious. Fluencio is a complete package that helps in preparing for presentations and interviews. It records and analyzes the speech to highlight mistakes and provides useful feedback to users. It tracks user's performance as it is designed considering the common needs of the users. The application also includes a meditation feature for the users to overcome the fear of public speaking.
The product also involve the use of VR Headset to provide hardware support. VR provides an opportunity to simlulate the environments of presentations and interview. It provides a great aid to users in practicing speech in real-world scenarios.

Our Product

Our Product

  • Allows you to record speech
  • Analyzes the audio input
  • Performs speech-to-text transcription
  • Highlights filler words
  • Calculates pitch and pace of audio
  • Shows the results
  • Displays progress in graph
  • Provides meditation feature
